Two Defenses
2019/08/26 - 2019/08/28

I wasn't ready to defend. I was so unprepared. My lab members warned me to know every facet of my thesis and I didn't listen! One of the examiners yielded his time and I think it was because he felt bad. I was tweaking my public lecture the day of. If my huntch is right, Blaine probably told the defense committee sonething like "Ahhhh the MSc doesn't mean much just give him a degree". I still got to drink of the goblet of knowledge so that's a plus.


Watching Chris give his talk was really great. We started our masters at the same time, and he was actually the first person from xMR to introduce himself to me. It was after the first TA meeting and I think the conversation went something like...

Chris: So you're also with Dr. Chronik... are you doing the one-year masters?
Me: Yup (I'm bad with new people)
Chris: Looks like we're the one-year guys.

We both ended up doing two-year theses.



The xMR Grad Students Camping Trip
2019/07/12 - 2019/07/15

This was my first time going camping. I'm more of a city person so camping really isn't my cup of tea. Although I'd never suggest to go camping myself, I enjoyed this trip.


Getting ready to make food.


I took a walk through the trails at Rondeau on the last day of our trip. Unfortunately, there was lot of mud since it had rained the days prior. Spicebush Trail was hell. Normally it would be easy to circumnavigate along the path but because of all the mud, I got stuck going one way and had to retrace my steps. And the bugs! Mosquitoes galore!


I didn't have the right equipment for camping. I brought a beach mat and a sleeping bag and it was uncomfortable. So uncomfortable that I got up at 5 am, walked around in the pitch black morning, and caught the sunrise over Lake Erie.
