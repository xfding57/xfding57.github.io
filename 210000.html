Biography

Xiao Fan is an Associate Scientist at the Canadian Light Source with over 8 years of experience specializing in medical imaging technologies. His expertise centers on CT and MRI applications for both clinical and non-destructive evaluation across diverse fields.

Xiao Fan's research combines imaging technqiues with advanced computational methods. He has develops deep-learning based image processing solutions and innovative post-image processing strategies. His technical proficiency extends to numerical modeling using finite element analysis and ensuring regulatory compliance with ASTM International and ISO standards. His collaborative approach has led to interdisciplinary partnerships, resulting in a strong publication record and he is recognized by multipla conerence awards.

Xiao Fan did his PhD studies in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Saskatchewan where he developed novel image processing technqiues to enhance the image contrast of hydrogel scaffolds. Previosly, he completd his MSc at Western University in London ON, focusing on numerical simulations of paramagnetic materials in magnetic resonance environments.
